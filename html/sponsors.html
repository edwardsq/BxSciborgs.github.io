  • The SciBorgs is an award-winning FIRST Robotics team originating from the Bronx High School of Science. FIRST is an international high school robotics competition built around coopertition (cooperation during competition) and gracious professionalism (empathy and respect for each other). We incorporate these values into our efforts by spreading our knowledge to younger generations and fostering an environment where young engineers can thrive.

    • FIRST robotics teams must spend thousands of dollars annually on costs for raw materials, equipment, travel expenses, and competition fees in order to participate.
    • We also seek mentors who specialize in mechanical engineering or computer science, who are willing to assist highly motivated and bright students at one of the most prestigious high schools in the nation.
